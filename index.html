Writing Stories

Russell Sisson began writing books in 2017. His first book, Of Universe and Ancestors, begins a trilogy, followed by Journey to Goldilocks and ending with On The Origin of Xe. It is a story that explains the basics of science as experienced from the perspective of an alien visitor to Earth. The first book introduces the character Xe who comes from a planet called Xeron in a far away galaxy. The other two books in the trilogy explore Xe's eventual return to Xeron. His fourth book, Imagine Waking Up, is a compilation of stories based on the music written and performed by Russell Sisson. His fifth book, The Ellie Mae, tells the story of a college graduate student named Allys who travels to Canyon de Chelly in northeastern Arizona to do some summer work at an archaeology site. While there, she discovers evidence of a small group of Basketmaker people who lived two thousand years ago. Allys begins a journey that takes her back to the time of the Basketmakers while uncovering the burial cist of an Ancestral Puebloan shaman.

Writing Songs and Recording Music

Russell Sisson has been playing and writing music for most of his life. Here, you will find his recorded music over the years, starting in 2015. The songs have all been written, performed and produced by Russell Sisson.

Discography

Russell Sisson started to record his music in 2015 and has tried to produce new music every year since. The recordings are very simply produced using a Yeti microphone and the recording software Audacity to capture his accoustic Martin guitar, vocals, harmonies and some special effect instruments.
