Writing Stories

Russell Sisson began writing books in 2017. His first book, Of Universe and Ancestors begins a trilogy, followed by Journey to Goldilocks and ending with On The Origin of Xe. It is a story based on science and science fiction, introducing the character Xe who lives on a planet called Xeron in a far away galaxy. His fourth book, Imagine Waking Up is a compilation of stories based on the music written and performed by Russell Sisson.

Writing Songs and Recording Music

Russell Sisson has been playing and writing music for most of his life. Here, you will find his recorded music over the years. The songs have all been written, performed and produced by Russell Sisson in his very unofficial home recording sessions using a single microphone and Audacity software.
